### Mailing Banner 
- **Overlay:** Dark overlay on the left (60–75% opacity) for text.
- **Headline:** Atyp Bold/ExtraBold, White, max 3–4 words.
- **Perex:** Atyp Regular, White or Grey, max 2 sentences.
- **Vertical Alignment:** The Heading and Perex text block must be vertically centered within the dark overlay or positioned in the upper half. Never place the main text at the very bottom.
- **CTA Button:** Summer Yellow (#FFD600), Atyp Bold, bottom left with generous margins.
- **Logo:** Partner logo top right (vendor). NO Sportega logo.
- **Image:** High-quality product or athlete.

### Social Media Banner Anatomy
- **Logo Sportega:** Top left, yellow text (~15% width for square).
- **CTA Button:** Summer Yellow, Atyp Bold, bottom left.
- **Safe Zones:** 60px from edges (Feed), 250px top/bottom (Stories).

- **Mailing:** Partner logo (Top Right). Sportega logo is OMITTED.
- **Discount Badge:** Summer Yellow background (`#FFD600`), Dark Blue text (`#070E30`), Atyp Bold/ExtraBold.
